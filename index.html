01. About Me

I'm a Full-stack Software Engineer with a passion for building robust, scalable web applications that blend performance with thoughtful user experience. I thrive at the intersection of backend architecture and frontend usability—crafting digital solutions that are as efficient under the hood as they are intuitive to use.

Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at RDAS Solutions, where I lead the development of a modular Laravel 12 monolith with multi-tenant support. My work focuses on implementing clean, maintainable code and delivering secure, role-based features that power dynamic enterprise-level applications.

Over the years, I've had the opportunity to contribute to startups, digital agencies, and growth-stage companies—solving real-world problems through full-stack engineering using tools like Django, React, Vue.js, and Laravel. I'm particularly drawn to systems with complex business logic, where I can apply my knowledge of authentication workflows, RBAC, and real-time data tracking to build high-impact features.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new frameworks, playing with data using Python libraries, deep in a good book—always learning, always building, or playing with musical instruments.
